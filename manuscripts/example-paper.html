Reflective and Answerable: Liberating the Entrepreneurial Imagination

Authors: Darcy Frankovich, R. G. Saylors, & Roy Suddaby

Status: Under review (OMJ Demonstration Version)

Abstract

This conceptual paper argues that entrepreneurial imagination has been captured by dominating logics— neoliberal, colonial, and patriarchal—limiting the possibilities for social transformation. We introduce the concept of imaginative reflexivity as a dialogic process that frees the entrepreneurial imagination from these constraints through answerable reflection.
